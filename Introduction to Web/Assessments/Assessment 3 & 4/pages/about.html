About This Website

This Website is purely entertainment. Everyone procrastinates, sure you feel a bit bad while doing it but we at WHW believe there are good and bad types of procrastination. One most common distraction method is social media, but most social media platforms are designed to get you addicted to scrolling to find more, you’ll spend so much time without realising it. Another common distraction is diving down rabbit holes of random videos, constantly telling yourself you will start work when each video ends. WHW believes that if you are going to procrastinate, you should engage your mind, it keeps you focused and active so that when that next push to work comes, you aren’t discouraged by how tired your mind is because you’ve kept it active and engaged. Whetheryou are at work, studying or just doing chores, you should come here to procrastinate, we will keep your brain excited and active with games and weird interactions, so when someone pushes you back to work you steam roll ahead.

A Bit About WHW

WHW means We Hate Work, We are a small group of freelancers who like to take time off work and build passion projects. We maintain our creativity for random projects by never receiving any revenue from them. While this is obviously ill advised, it makes it so it doesn't feel like work and we can go at our own pace. Check out our socials to see some of our other projects.
